About Us

Vedasamskriti Yoga Foundation is a Non-Profit organization, whose aim is to awaken the human consciousness and empower mankind to realize the potential which lies within each and every human being. The organization works to create beautiful world, to develop inner and outer dimensions of students and citizens to live life happily.

Vedasamskriti Yoga Foundation uses the wisdom of ancient Vedic knowledge methods, yogic practices in to modern day education and to life style.

Vedasamskriti Yoga Foundation trains people to become more productive in life through programs. We believe that traditional approaches to the health care are much powerful and better.

Our approach, towards Physical Management, Mental Management, Social Management, Emotional Management and Spiritual Managements, which emphasizes prevention and good health promotion, is much more proactive.

By helping citizens to change their behaviour patterns and choose more healthy lifestyles, Vedasamskriti Yoga Foundation will reduce governments and people’s health care expenditures drastically. The position of India’s health care crisis, coupled with current demographic changes, threatens not only exacerbate the crisis, but further erode worker’s productivity as well. These environmental factors coupled with the local competitive situation signal a favourable opportunity in this market. We feel that the time is right for forming M/s. Vedasamskriti Yoga Foundation.

Vedasamskriti Yoga Foundation brings holistic vedic based learning methods for the children. Foundation works towards the aim of changing the situation that the learning tends to play and joy rather than stress learning. Children get benefitted by attending Vedic Based Memory Technique Program – (VBMTP) i.e., 30 days Residential camp, which boosts the children to complete their 1 year syllabus within 1 month.

Vedasamskriti Yoga Foundation advanced teaching method is heart-oriented, spiritually inspiring, yet grounded in a deep knowledge of optimal body alignment in the poses. Learning process encourage students to listen to their body, respect its wisdom, and to progress at their own pace. Adults get benefitted with Basic Yoga Course (15 days, Non–Residential Program) teaches the science of joyful living. Our advanced Yoga course can reverse the ageing process, cure all diseases and open up incredible creativity.

Deep relaxation is taught in each class with science of non-doing process. The intention of the Vedasamskriti Yoga Foundation is to assist students in the development of physical, mental, and spiritual well-being, a truly priceless health advantage.
